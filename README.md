## About Me

I lead a digitalization team in a Japanese corporation, focused on building practical systems with modern technologies like Go, TypeScript, Vue, and React.

Even in a management role, I like to keep coding—partly for research, partly to test ideas, and mostly to stay connected to the real needs of the team and the work itself.

My work usually sits between operations, software, and industrial IoT: internal tools, dashboards, automation, ERP-related systems, and data flows that help the factory run better.

---

## What I Work On

- Factory digitalization and smart manufacturing initiatives.
- Full-stack internal tools for operations and production teams.
- Industrial IoT systems, monitoring, and real-time dashboards.
- Process automation, reporting, and data-driven decision support.
- Team leadership, solution evaluation, and implementation strategy.
